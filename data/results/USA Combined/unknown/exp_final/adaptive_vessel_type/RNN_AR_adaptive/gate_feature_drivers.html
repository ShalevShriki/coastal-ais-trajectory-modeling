Adaptive gate — feature drivers

n=15,000 test windows. The gate sees encoder hidden states (not raw AIS features). This report correlates observable motion summaries with α to interpret what the gate prefers.

Takeaways
  • Strongest Spearman association overall: std_sog (max |r|=0.243).
  • Best RF driver of long-vs-short preference (α_24h−α_9h): anchor_lat.
  • Gate still puts most mass on 18–24h contexts on average; motion features modulate this only mildly.
  • Among vessel classes, passenger leans most on 24h (α_24h=0.388).
